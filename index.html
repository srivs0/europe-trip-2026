Prague

Jul 28–31 · 3 nights · The Julius Prague

Czech Food Cheat Sheet

Svíčková — beef sirloin in cream sauce with cranberries + bread dumplings. The national dish.
Guláš — rich, dark goulash, often served in a bread bowl.
Vepřové koleno — roasted pork knuckle. Massive, shareable.
Smažený sýr — deep-fried cheese with tartar sauce. Czech comfort food.
Kolache — traditional pastries (quark, poppy seed, plum jam). The real Czech pastry — NOT trdelník.
Beer — Pilsner Urquell, Budvar, Kozel. Half-litre in a pub: CZK 40–70 (under $3).

Bern

Aug 2–5 · 3 nights · Hotel Savoy Bern

Swiss Food Warning

Switzerland is expensive. A restaurant meal runs CHF 25–45/person (~$28–50). Budget accordingly. The upside: the quality is consistently high, and fondue in its homeland is a must-do experience.
